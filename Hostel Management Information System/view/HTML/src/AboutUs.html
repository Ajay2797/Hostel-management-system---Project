About our college

Created collaboratively by members of the RISD community, NEXT, RISD’s seven-year strategic plan (PDF), proposes how we intend to educate students for the future and bring our current creative practices to bear on today’s most critical social, political and environmental challenges.

The mission of CEM college of engineering and technology, through its college and museum, is to educate its students and the public in the creation and appreciation of works of art and design, to discover and transmit knowledge and to make lasting contributions to a global society through critical thinking, scholarship and innovation.
